The goal is simple: identify whether the displayed **Bible book** belongs to the **Old Testament** or the **New Testament**.

Game Flow

  • Start: Click "Start Game" to begin the challenge.
  • The Challenge: For each level, you must correctly identify **12 books**.
  • Timing: Once the level starts, the **countdown timer** at the top right begins. This timer runs continuously for the entire level!
  • Scoring: Each correct answer earns you **2 points**.

Rules & Difficulty

You must complete the 12 books before the time runs out, or the game ends!

  • Difficulty: Set your challenge level in the **Settings** menu. Higher difficulty means less time to complete the 12 selections.
  • Game Over: The game ends if you choose the wrong testament or if the timer reaches zero.

Good luck and enjoy the challenge!
